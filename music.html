Killing Time : The Music.

Our short animation relies on a very atmospheric soundtrack which has to provide specific feelings at critical moments of the story.

John Laval is the talented musician and composer
behind this highly important task. It is said that music is at least 50 percent of what is perceived by the audience of a movie.
Achieving the right balance between supporting the images and carrying the narrative is a difficult task.
Killing Time aims to sustain and direct the narrative through the soundtrack, demanding subtlety and strength to make this happen.
