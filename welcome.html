Killing Time: The Animation.

This is the home of the first story we decided to animate and produce whilst foolishly trying to live a normal life and not resemble the undead. Unfortunately we now both look like extras from The Addams Family, but looking on the bright side the project is coming along nicely… This website will provide a range of information about our short animation so that you can get a feel for the character and set designs, and for how the idea has evolved to create the ambience for Killing Time.

The animation has been driven entirely by our narrative needs rather than the technical brilliance of current software, so please don’t expect a blow by blow account of how we remedied technical issues or how we created the rain. It’s all about the story. Now that the site is presentable it will be updated regularly until completion of the animated short.

We might even add a few bells and whistles now that we can see the welcoming glow of light at the end of the tunnel and the approach of spare time... Well unallocated rather then spare… ‘Behind the scenes’ features are not yet available. They’re being written in between convincing our loved ones why writing them is more important than being a reasonable human…
